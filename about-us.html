About Us

About Liveplay

Liveplay is a free-to-use service that provides a simple, user-friendly interface to find and watch publicly available live television streams from the internet. Our goal is to make a wide variety of global content easily accessible in one place.

Our Content Sources

Liveplay functions as a search engine and directory, indexing links to streams from various third-party sources. This includes public YouTube live streams, channels from the community-maintained IPTV-org database on GitHub, and other publicly accessible streams found on the web.

Legal Disclaimer

Liveplay does not host, control, or own any of the content streamed on our platform. All streams are the property of their respective owners. We are not affiliated with, nor endorsed by, any of the broadcasters whose streams may appear on our site. The responsibility for the legality and content of the streams lies entirely with the original sources.
