Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ! Here, you'll find answers to common questions about Liveplay, including how to watch free live TV, our privacy practices, and tips to enhance your experience. Looking for a specific station or just want to discover something new? Liveplay makes it easy.

What is Liveplay's role? expand_more

Liveplay acts as a search engine and directory. We do not host or broadcast any content ourselves. Our service finds and organizes links to publicly available TV streams from the internet to provide a convenient viewing experience.

Is it legal to watch channels on Liveplay? expand_more

We provide links to streams that are already publicly available online. However, the legality of accessing specific content can vary by region. It is the user's responsibility to ensure they are complying with the copyright laws in their country.

Why is an account required to watch? expand_more

A free account is required to access our platform. This helps us maintain a stable and secure service for all users and allows for basic personalization. Your information is used only for core functions like logging in.

What happens to my data if I delete my account? expand_more

If you choose to delete your account, all of your personal information (name, email, username) will be permanently removed from our active databases, as stated in our Privacy Policy.

Why is a channel unavailable or not working? expand_more

Because we link to external sources, we do not control the availability of the streams. If a channel is down, the issue lies with the original provider. We recommend trying again later, as these disruptions are often temporary.
