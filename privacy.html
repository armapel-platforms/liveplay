Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 8, 2025

Liveplay ("us", "we", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This page informs you of our policies regarding your data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

To create an account, you provide us with your First Name, Last Name, Username, and Email Address. We may also collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (like browser type and pages visited) to improve our service. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to provide and maintain the Service. This includes creating your account, allowing you to log in, personalizing your experience (e.g., greetings), and for essential account-related communication like password resets.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account information is securely stored via our backend provider, Supabase. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no internet service can be 100% secure.

4. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your personal information. You can access, update, or permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from your "My Account" page.

5. Cookies

We use cookies only for essential functions, such as keeping you logged in. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

6. Third-Party Services and Links

The core of our service is linking to third-party content. When you watch a stream, you are interacting with content served from external sources. We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to be aware of their policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
